About

Andrew Berman has always been fascinated by art and form. This fascination is not only evident in his professional life as an architect and urban designer based in Cape Town, South Africa, but in his two greatest passions: fine art and photography.

Andrew most often paints life drawings, as he finds the body interesting and enjoys the challenge of illustrating it. Though Andrew has painted in both oils and watercolour, his favourite medium is pastels. Andrew has painted primarily with The Artist's Collective, a Cape Town community art group Andrew has supported, managed and developed for many years. This has cemented his place in the art community.

Rarely without a camera, Andrew has long been fascinated by the camera and its ability to capture the world. While Andrew favours Leica cameras, he experiments with a wide variety of cameras in both black and white. Though he has not yet exhibited his work, as he has done with his life drawings, his rich collection of photographs is testament to his passion for the medium.
